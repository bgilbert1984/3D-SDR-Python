Welcome!   Project website: kiwisdr.com    Here are some tips:
  • Show and hide the panels by using the circled arrows at the top right corner.
  • Most major browsers should work fine (except for Internet Explorer).
  • There is no support for small-screen mobile devices currently.
  • You can click and/or drag almost anywhere on the page to change settings.
  • Enter a numeric frequency (in kHz) in the box at right (top left corner).
  • Or use the "select band" menu to jump to a pre-defined band.
  • Use the zoom icons to control the waterfall span.
  • Tune by clicking on the waterfall, spectrum or the multi-colored station labels.
  • Control-shift or alt-shift click in the waterfall to lookup frequency in online databases.
  • Control or alt click to page spectrum down and up in frequency.
  • Adjust the "WF floor/min" slider for best waterfall colors.
  • Type 'h' or '?' to see the list of keyboard shortcuts.
  • See the Operating information page and KiwiSDR Forum.
